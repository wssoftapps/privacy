Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Adwiyati – Medicine Reminder ("the app"), developed by WS SoftApps, handles your information.

1. What the App Does

Adwiyati is a medicine reminder and scheduling utility app. It helps you track medicines, set reminders, and check medicine safety using AI. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, or replace professional medical advice.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

All your medicine data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Medicine names, dosages, and schedules
  • Photos of medicine boxes (taken with your camera)
  • Dose history and reminder logs

This data is never uploaded to any server and is never shared with third parties.

3. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your camera so you can take a photo of a medicine box. The photo is analyzed by AI to automatically fill in the medicine name and details. Photos are saved only on your device.

4. AI Safety Checker (Gemini AI)

When you use the "Ask Me" feature to check medicine safety, only the medicine name or your typed question is sent to Google's Gemini AI service. No personal information, photos, or health history is included in these requests.

Google's Gemini AI is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)

If the app crashes, an automatic technical error report is sent to the developer via Firebase Crashlytics (by Google). These reports contain only technical information (device type, Android version, error details) — no medicine data or personal information is included.

6. Analytics (Firebase Analytics)

The app uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (e.g., which screens are visited, how often the app is opened). This helps improve the app. No personal or health data is collected.

7. Notifications & Alarms

The app schedules local reminders and notifications on your device. These are processed entirely on-device and no notification content is transmitted externally.

8. Family Alerts (WhatsApp / SMS)

If you choose to send a family alert for a missed dose, the app opens WhatsApp or SMS with a pre-written message. This is done through your own WhatsApp/SMS app — the developer does not receive or store any of this information.

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
